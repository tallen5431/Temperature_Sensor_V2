Quick Start

Box to live readings in ~10 minutes.

Runs on your own computer — no cloud, no account, no subscription. You flash it in your browser in one click; your readings stay on your machine.

  1. Assemble it

    Solder the parts to the board (or plug them into the headers on the Plug-Together kit). Full illustrated steps at the guide QR below.

  2. Flash it (browser)

    Open the flash QR in Chrome/Edge, plug in the SuperMini's USB-C with the power switch OFF, and click Flash. Native USB — no drivers, no toolchain.

  3. Power it on

    Add your 18650 and flip the switch ON (or just stay on USB). The status LED blinks — the probe is awake and looking for Wi-Fi.

  4. Install the free hub

    On a computer you keep on (PC, mini-PC, or NAS), run the hub app and open http://localhost:8080. It's a free download — nothing to buy.

  5. Point the probe at your Wi-Fi

    On your phone, join the probe's network Setpoint-XXXXXX (2.4 GHz). A setup page opens — pick your home Wi-Fi and save.

  6. Watch it appear

    Within a minute the probe shows up on your dashboard with a live temperature. Set a high/low alert and you're done.

If something's off

  • Reads −127 or blank? The sensor's pull-up isn't seated — re-check that connection.
  • Won't join Wi-Fi? It's 2.4 GHz only — pick a 2.4 GHz network, not 5 GHz.
  • Nothing on the dashboard? Make sure the hub computer and the probe are on the same network.

You'll need: a computer left on 24/7 on your network to run the free hub, and a 2.4 GHz Wi-Fi network. One hub handles many probes.

🔋 Battery not included — add one reputable 18650 (flat-top or protected, ~2500–3500 mAh; the TP4056 handles protection). Avoid "ultra-high-mAh" fakes. Mind polarity, never short the terminals, charge only through the TP4056.
