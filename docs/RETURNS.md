# Returns & Refunds

_Last updated: 2026_

> **Note for the maker/reseller:** Setpoint is a white-label product. Before you
> ship, replace **Setpoint**/**Setpoint** with your product names, confirm
> the return window and who pays return shipping fit your business and local law,
> and update the support link (`https://example.com/support`) to match your
> `branding` config.

We want you to be happy with your purchase. If something is not right, here is
how returns and refunds work. This policy is for direct online consumer sales;
if you bought from a reseller, their policy may also apply.

## 30-day return window

You may return most hardware within **30 days of delivery** for a refund or
exchange. Contact us first at **https://example.com/support** to start a return —
do not ship anything back without a return authorization.

## Condition of returned items

- **Unused / like-new returns (change of mind):** items must be in original,
  resellable condition with all parts, cables, and packaging. We may deduct a
  reasonable amount for missing items or damage beyond normal inspection. For
  change-of-mind returns, **you pay return shipping**; original shipping is
  non-refundable.
- **Defective or dead-on-arrival items:** if the hardware is faulty, we cover
  return shipping and refund or replace it in full. A defect after the 30-day
  window is handled under the **Limited Hardware Warranty** (`docs/WARRANTY.md`),
  which runs for one year.

Because Setpoint is **local-first with no cloud and no account**, there is no
subscription to cancel and no server-side data to delete — anything the software
stored lives only on your PC (see `PRIVACY.md`).

## What is not returnable

- Items damaged by misuse, accident, liquid, or wrong power (see the warranty
  exclusions).
- Items missing labels/QR/serials or returned without proof of purchase.
- Clearance or "as-is" items marked non-returnable at the time of sale.

## How to return

1. **Start a return** at **https://example.com/support** within 30 days. Include
   your order number, the probe ID(s), and the reason (defective vs. change of
   mind). See `SUPPORT.md` for the full list of details to include.
2. **Get a return authorization** and shipping instructions from us. Pack the
   item safely, ideally in its original box.
3. **Ship it back** using the method we provide. For defective items we send a
   prepaid label; for change-of-mind returns you arrange and pay for shipping.
4. **Refund.** After we receive and inspect the item, we issue your refund to the
   original payment method, typically within a few business days (your bank may
   take longer to post it). Exchanges ship once the return is received.

## Your legal rights

This policy is in addition to any rights you have under your local
consumer-protection law, including any statutory right to return faulty goods.
Nothing here limits a right that cannot be waived.

Questions? Contact **https://example.com/support**.
