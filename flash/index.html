Flash your Setpoint

Put the latest firmware on an ESP32-C3 straight from your browser — no Arduino IDE, no toolchain, no account. firmware v2.4.1

You'll need: desktop Chrome, Edge, or Opera · an ESP32-C3 board on a USB-C data cable (not charge-only).

  1. Plug the board into a USB port on this computer (power switch ON).
  2. Click Flash Setpoint and pick the board's serial port. If none appears, try another cable (many are charge-only) or hold BOOT while connecting.
  3. When it finishes, follow the setup guide: join the Setpoint-XXXXXX Wi-Fi and point it at your dashboard.
This browser can't flash over USB. Open the page in Chrome, Edge, or Opera on a desktop — Web Serial isn't available in Safari, Firefox, or on mobile. You can also flash by hand with arduino-cli.
