Flash your ThermaProbe

Put the firmware on an ESP32 board straight from your browser — no Arduino IDE, no toolchain, no installs.

You'll need: a desktop Chrome, Edge, or Opera browser, and an ESP32 board connected to this computer with a USB-C data cable.

  1. Plug the ESP32 board into a USB port on this computer.
  2. Click Flash ThermaProbe below and pick the board's serial port when prompted. (If nothing appears, use a different USB cable — many are charge-only — or install the CP210x / CH340 USB-serial driver for your board.)
  3. Wait for it to finish, then unplug and follow the setup guide: join the probe's ThermaProbe-XXXXXX Wi-Fi and point it at your ThermaHub.
This browser can't flash over USB. Open this page in Chrome, Edge, or Opera on a desktop (Web Serial isn't available in Safari, Firefox, or on mobile). You can always flash manually with arduino-cli instead — see firmware/README.md.
